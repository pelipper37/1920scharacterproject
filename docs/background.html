Family Name

  • Mr. Horaz has a name that may or may not be of note in our efforts to neutralize the threat that he may pose.
  • Hugo is a Latin variant of the name Hugh, itself being an English variant of the name Hubert, meaning bright-minded. Fitting considering the troubles he has caused us, and our inability to apprehend him.
  • Paul is a Latin name meaning small. Considering how influential he has become without being in the spotlight often, it is a good descriptor of Mr. Horaz's personality.
  • Horaz is a variant of Horace, a Latin name. It means "keeper of the hours".
