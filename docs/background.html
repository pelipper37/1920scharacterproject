Personal Background

Hugo Paul Horaz was born into the middle-class on 14 June 1897 in Los Angeles, California, during the height of the Gilded Age. His father was a banker, and his mother a schoolteacher. Intelligence has yet to be gathered on those two. He was consistently at the top of his class from an early age, and one could consider him a prodigy in mathematics. Mr. Horaz was accepted into Harvard at the age of 16 and graduated in 1918. Mr. Horaz served in the First World War for 7 weeks before war ended in 1918. He was an ally of James M. Cox of Ohio and was an advisor in his 1920 run for the presidency, but it should be noted that Mr. Horaz's views are more conservative than the progressive and somewhat radical ideologies that the Democratic party embraced after 1884. He currently resides in Los Angeles County.
